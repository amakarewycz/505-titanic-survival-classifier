Additional EDA

Looking at feature importance, it seems features like being a youthful female is a strong predictor at surviving the Titanic disaster. Added a synthetic feature, "young female", F(young female) = F(female)==true and (F(Age (20, 28]) == 1 or F(Age (28, 38]) == 1) and re-trained several models to see if it made any difference in the prediction outcomes.

Also looked at a coupld of variant for "young female". Looked at "young male", "older male", "young female" in "Class 3". All of these were no good, and worsened the model.
